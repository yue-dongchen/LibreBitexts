Welcome to LibreBitexts!

The LibreBitexts project assembles bitexts (parallel texts) from public domain sources and makes them available under a Creative Commons licence, with the aim of eventually building a sizeable collection of various works between a plethora of language pairs.

Go to the "Texts" page for downloads. Texts are currently available in A4 PDF format. To contribute, contact Dongchen Yue.
